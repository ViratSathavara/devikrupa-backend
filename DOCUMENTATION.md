### 🎯 Purpose of Landing Page

To **attract visitors**, explain what the business does, and **convert visitors into inquiries or customers**.

---

### 1.1 Smart Hero Section

**What it does**

* Shows the company name, tagline, and main services clearly
* Highlights 2–3 key selling points

**UX Flow**

* User lands on site
* Immediately understands:

  * What the company sells
  * Who it is for
  * Why they should trust it

---

### 1.2 Featured Products Section

**What it does**

* Shows top or popular products
* Displays product image, short name, and category

**UX Flow**

* User scrolls
* Sees products visually
* Clicks to explore product details

---

### 1.3 Company Strength Highlights

**What it shows**

* Years of experience
* Number of products
* Number of satisfied customers

**Why**

* Builds trust without reading long text

---

### 1.4 Customer Testimonials

**What it does**

* Shows customer feedback with name and rating

**UX Flow**

* User reads real opinions
* Confidence increases before inquiry

---

### 1.5 Inquiry / Contact Section

**What it does**

* Simple form:

  * Name
  * Phone / Email
  * Product interest
  * Message

**UX Flow**

* User submits inquiry
* Business gets a lead

### 🎯 Purpose of Admin Panel

To **control the entire system**, monitor data, and manage products & users.

---

## 3.1 Admin Dashboard (Overview Screen)

**What it shows at a glance**

* Total products
* Available stock count
* Out-of-stock products
* Total inquiries
* Recent activity

**Visual Elements**

* Cards
* Charts
* Small summary blocks

---

## 📊 3.2 Charts & Analytics (Admin Only)

**Suggested charts**

* Products by category (pie chart)
* Monthly inquiries (bar chart)
* Stock availability (progress chart)

**Why**

* Admin understands business health quickly
* No need to read tables

---

## 📦 3.3 Product Management (Your Requested Feature – Detailed)

### 🔹 Product List View

Each product row shows:

* Product name
* Category
* Total stock count
* Available stock
* Sold quantity
* Status (Available / Low stock / Out of stock)

---

### 🔹 Manual Stock Control (Phase 1 – Your Current Plan)

**What admin can do**

* Increase product quantity (new stock added)
* Decrease product quantity (product sold manually)

**Example Flow**

1. Product total stock = 50
2. Admin marks **5 products sold**
3. Available stock becomes **45**

This is **manual control**, perfect for now.

---

### 🔹 Automatic Stock Control (Phase 2 – Future)

Later enhancement:

* When an order is confirmed:

  * Stock automatically decreases
* If stock reaches zero:

  * Product marked as “Out of stock”

---

### 🔹 Stock History Log

**What it tracks**

* Date
* Product name
* Quantity increased or decreased
* Reason (sold / damaged / new stock)

---

## 3.4 Inquiry Management

**Admin can**

* View all inquiries
* Assign status:

  * New
  * Contacted
  * Closed
* Add internal notes

---

## 3.5 User Management

**Admin can**

* View all registered users
* Block / unblock users
* See inquiry history per user

---

## 3.6 Notifications (Optional but Professional)

**Admin gets notified when**

* Stock is low
* New inquiry arrives
* Product goes out of stock

---

## 🧱 4. Future-Proof Enhancements (Later Stages)

* Role-based access (Admin, Staff)
* Export reports (PDF / Excel)
* Multi-language support
* Dark / Light mode
* Activity audit logs
